Difference between <section>,<div>,<article> tag:
<section>
The <section> element represents a generic section of a document or application,grouping of content
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<div>
The <div> element has no special meaning at all.
It can be used with the class, lang, and title attributes to mark up semantics common to a group of consecutive elements
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
<article>
The <article> element represents a independent item section of content. It must have a <h1> tag for title.forum post, magazine article,newspaper article
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
